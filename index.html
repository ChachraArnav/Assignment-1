Die For You

By The Weeknd

About the song:

"Die For You" stands out as a deeply emotional piece from The Weeknd's "Starboy" album. The song melds earnest lyrics with a moody R&B melody, capturing the essence of sacrificial love. It's a sonic journey through the highs and lows of devotion, and The Weeknd's passionate delivery makes it a memorable anthem of love's enduring power.

The Weeknd, born Abel Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer known for revolutionizing R&B with his ethereal voice and dark, poignant lyrics. Breaking onto the scene with anonymous uploads that stirred curiosity, he has since become a global sensation. His work spans haunting ballads to upbeat hits, earning him multiple accolades and a dedicated fan base.
